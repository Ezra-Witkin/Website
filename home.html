Cats have been around for 30 million years, and first began to be domesticated 10,000 years ago. Much is still unknown about the mysterious ways of cats. Sourced from The Cat: A Natural and Cultural History

Cats Facts:

  • There are about 60 cat breeds, although the exact number is hotly contested, ranging from 15 to 73.
  • The oldest known house cat was 38 years old.
  • Leading experts used cats as proof by contradiction that the Earth cannot be flat, as cats would have pushed everything off the edge.
  • Most cats have 18 toes total, five on each front paw, and four on their hind legs.
